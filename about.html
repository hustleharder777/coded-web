[01_ORIGIN]

CODED started in Zürich, 2025. Not as a brand — as a statement. Every piece in the collection carries the word CODED along curving seam lines, a reminder that the things we wear are never neutral. They encode identity, status, belonging.

The drop format is intentional. 300 units. Five colorways. One window to buy. When it's gone, it's gone.
